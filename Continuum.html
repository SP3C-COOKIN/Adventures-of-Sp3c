Continuum Headquarters

[Continuum.co](http://continuum.co/) is a multiversal agency established within the Betweenway — the infinite passage through which all universes converge, intersect, and remain apart.

It exists for the maintenance of cosmic stability, the retrieval and preservation of ancient texts, artifacts, and lost histories, and the safeguarding of realities that have been fractured, abandoned, or erased.

The organization was founded in the aftermath of the [Redacted] War, at the dawn of the Birogl Era — a turning point marked by the collapse of order across multiple dimensions.

Its formation was not of ambition, but necessity: to prevent the erosion of existence, to preserve what would otherwise be forgotten, and to ensure that even the weakest or dying universes retain their memory.
